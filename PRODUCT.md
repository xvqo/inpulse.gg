# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Polish gamers aged 16–28 considering joining a FiveM GTA Roleplay server. They arrive via Discord links, YouTube content, or word of mouth. When they land, they're evaluating credibility: is this server active, professional, worth downloading and learning? They want social proof fast, then a path to join.

## Product Purpose

Convert landing page visitors into Discord members and active server players. The site exists to establish INPULSE as the premier Polish FiveM RP server — credible, active, and worth their time. Success is a Discord join or a direct server connection.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic, intense, elite. The brand borrows from game trailers and action film titles — not a fun cartoon server, not a chaotic community site. It should feel like stepping into something serious.

## Anti-references

Inferred — not confirmed by user. Likely: generic Minecraft/garry's mod server pages (bright, pixelated, chaotic), neon-on-black "gamer" clichés, amateurish template sites with stock photos and rainbow gradients.

## Design Principles

1. **Credibility first.** Every element should signal that real people play here and the server is maintained. Stats, live player count, and community size are the trust engine.
2. **Conversion is the only job.** Every section exists to move the visitor closer to Discord or the server connection. No filler, no decorative dead ends.
3. **Cinematic restraint.** The intro and motion should feel like a trailer, not a slot machine. One impressive moment beats five competing animations.
4. **Polish content, global craft.** The language is Polish but the visual bar should match international gaming brands (Rockstar, Riot, Bungie landing pages).
5. **Community > features.** Players join servers for people, not systems. Lead with social proof, atmosphere, and belonging — not a feature list.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Not specified. Assumed WCAG AA as baseline. Polish-language content only.
